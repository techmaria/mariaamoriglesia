Phase 01 · Diagnosis
Identifying the Revenue Leak
The business was running Facebook Lead Ads but had no structured CRM. Leads were collected in a spreadsheet, manually emailed days later—sometimes never. An audit revealed over 60% of inquiries were never followed up past the first touchpoint.
Phase 02 · Architecture
Building the Capture Pipeline
Integrated Facebook Lead Ads directly into GoHighLevel CRM via webhook. Every new lead was automatically tagged by course interest, assigned to a pipeline stage, and queued for a follow-up sequence—all within seconds of form submission.
Phase 03 · Automation
Deploying the Follow-Up Engine
Designed a 5-step email + SMS nurture sequence triggered immediately on lead capture. Messages were personalized by course tag and timed to hit at peak engagement windows. A ClickUp task was also auto-created so the sales team received real-time notifications.
Phase 04 · Results
30% Conversion Lift in 60 Days
Within 60 days, the enrollment rate improved by 30%. Lead response time dropped from an average of 3 hours to under 5 minutes. Zero leads were lost to manual oversight. The system now runs without any administrative intervention.
