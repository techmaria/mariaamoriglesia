Phase 01 · Diagnosis
Identifying the Revenue Leak
The business was running Facebook Lead Ads with no structured CRM. Leads collected in spreadsheets, emailed days later — sometimes never. An audit revealed over 60% of inquiries never received a second follow-up touchpoint.
CRM Audit Lead Flow Mapping Gap Analysis
Phase 02 · Architecture
Building the Capture Pipeline
Integrated Facebook Lead Ads directly into GoHighLevel CRM via webhook. Every new lead was automatically tagged by course interest, assigned to a pipeline stage, and queued for follow-up — all within seconds of form submission.
GoHighLevel Webhook Integration Pipeline Setup
Phase 03 · Automation
Deploying the Follow-Up Engine
Designed a 5-step Email + SMS nurture sequence triggered immediately on lead capture. Messages personalized by course tag, timed to hit peak engagement windows. ClickUp tasks auto-created for real-time team notifications.
Email Sequences SMS Automation ClickUp Tasks
Phase 04 · Results
+30% Conversion Lift in 60 Days
Enrollment rate improved 30% within 60 days. Lead response time dropped from 3+ hours to under 5 minutes. Zero leads lost to manual oversight. The system ran without any administrative intervention.
Key Result
From 3-hour manual response → 5-minute automated response. Every lead captured, categorized, and followed up without a single manual step.
Phase 05 · Optimization & A/B Testing
Refining the System for Higher Yield
After baseline results were established, A/B testing was introduced across email subject lines, SMS send times, and call-to-action copy. Two variants were run simultaneously, with performance tracked directly inside GHL analytics.
A/B Email Subject Lines SMS Timing Tests CTA Copy Variants
Test Insight
SMS sent within the first 2 minutes outperformed 30-minute delayed sends by 22% in open rate. Subject line personalization increased email clicks by 18%.
Phase 06 · Continuous Management
Keeping the System Performing Over Time
Monthly performance reviews to audit pipeline health, sequence deliverability, and conversion drop-off points. Automation logic updated as the offer and audience evolved. New lead sources integrated without rebuilding the core architecture.
