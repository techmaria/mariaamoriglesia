System Build Case Study 01
GHL + Zapier + HubSpot

Social Media → Lead Capture → CRM → Follow-up Automation

Problem

No system for capturing and nurturing leads from social media. Inquiries were coming in through Facebook but were tracked manually—leading to slow response times, lost contacts, and zero follow-up consistency.

Solution

Built an automated pipeline using GoHighLevel and Zapier that captures leads from Meta Ads and forms, tags them in a CRM, triggers an immediate email or SMS response, and creates a follow-up task in ClickUp—all without manual input.

Results

Response time reduced from hours to under 5 minutes

All leads automatically tagged and organized in CRM pipeline

Follow-up sequences triggered automatically—zero manual sending

Team tasks auto-created in ClickUp on each new lead trigger
